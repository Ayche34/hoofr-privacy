Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a trip?

Tap "Create Trip" from the My Trips screen. Enter your trip name, dates, and location. You'll get a 6-digit code to share with your group.

How do I join a trip?

Tap "Join Trip" and enter the 6-digit code shared by the trip host. You'll be added to the trip and can see all the details instantly.

How do I add players?

The trip host can add players from the Players tab — either one by one or by importing a CSV file. Players who join with a trip code are automatically added to the roster.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Go to your Profile (tap the person icon on My Trips), scroll down, and tap "Delete Account." This permanently removes your account and all associated data.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your trip data is only visible to members who join with the trip code. We do not sell or share your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.
